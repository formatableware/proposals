What You Are Buying

Why Bugambilia

Bugambilia by G.E.T. is the commercial standard for high-volume operations. Every piece is built to last — and priced to save you money over time.

Truly Unbreakable
G.E.T. composite construction withstands commercial kitchens, high-volume service, and repeated drops — no chips, no cracks, no replacements.
Manufacturer Warranty
Every piece is backed by a full manufacturer warranty against defects. Not a promise — a documented guarantee on every unit in your order.
Temperature Retention
Designed to hold both heat and cold significantly longer than ceramic or porcelain — food arrives at the right temperature, every time.
NSF Certified
Meets NSF commercial kitchen standards. Dishwasher safe, BPA-free, and compliant with health & safety requirements for food service operations.
Lower Cost Over Time
Ceramic breaks. Replace a plate twice and you have already spent more than this. One purchase, zero attrition — your cost-per-service drops every month.
