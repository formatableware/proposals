What You Are Buying

Why Bugambilia

Bugambilia by G.E.T. is the commercial standard for high-volume operations. Every piece is built to last — and priced to save you money over time.

Truly Unbreakable
G.E.T. cast aluminium withstands commercial kitchens, high-volume service, and repeated drops — no chips, no cracks, no replacements.
Manufacturer Warranty
Every piece is backed by a full manufacturer warranty against defects — documented on every unit in your order.
Temperature Retention
Holds heat significantly longer than ceramic or fiberglass — food arrives at the right temperature every time.
NSF Certified
Meets NSF commercial kitchen standards. Dishwasher safe, BPA-free, compliant with food service health & safety requirements.
Lower Cost Over Time
Ceramic and fiberglass trays break and scratch. One Bugambilia purchase, zero attrition — cost-per-service drops every month.
