ADVANCE Tier
ROYAL
WMF's flagship stemware for professional hospitality — the standard choice for restaurant wine programs worldwide. Royal features a classically proportioned bowl, a slender stem of exceptional stability, and machine-blown crystal-clear glass that presents colour, clarity, and effervescence with precision. Its slightly heavier weight and wide base make it resilient for high-turnover service environments without sacrificing elegance.
Style: Classic stem
Glass: Machine-blown soda-lime
Dishwasher: Safe
Pack: 6 per box
Best for: Restaurant, tasting room, events
Tier: WMF ADVANCE
SUPREME Tier
DIVINE
WMF's premium glassware line for establishments where the glass is part of the guest experience. Divine is noticeably thinner and lighter than Royal, with a more elongated bowl profile that directs aromas toward the nose — ideal for guided wine tastings, premium pours, and cellar experiences. The refined silhouette photographs beautifully, making it a natural choice for winery retail and tasting room branding.
Style: Fine stem, elongated bowl
Glass: Machine-blown fine glass
Dishwasher: Safe
Pack: 6 per box
Best for: Tastings, premium pours, retail gift
Tier: WMF SUPREME

Why WMF Glassware — Talking Points for Your Team

01
Made in Germany · Since 1853
WMF has been producing table culture products in Germany for over 170 years. That heritage resonates with wine consumers who care about provenance — the same guests who care where their grapes come from will notice that the glass was made with the same commitment to craft.
02
Dishwasher Safe — Every Single SKU
Both Royal and Divine are fully dishwasher safe at commercial temperatures. Staff can run the glasses through your commercial machine without fear of thermal shock, cloudiness, or stem damage — critical during high-volume tastings and events.
03
Stem Stability · Designed for Service
Royal's base-to-bowl ratio is engineered for service stability — a wider base footprint means fewer knockovers on barn-board tables, uneven stone patios, and outdoor tasting decks. A practical design decision made for the real hospitality environment.
04
Consistent Availability · No Hunt Required
WMF maintains consistent stock availability across Royal and Divine — these are core catalog items, not seasonal specials. When you need to replace a box after a busy weekend, the glass you reorder will match exactly what's already on your shelf.
05
Aroma-Optimised Bowl Geometry
Divine's elongated bowl and tapered rim channel volatile aromatic compounds directly to the nose. For guided tastings where you're asking guests to engage their senses, the glass shape is doing real work. Use this in your tasting notes and flight descriptions.
06
BC Pour Compliance Built In
The measured-fill variants (0.1L / 0.2L / 0.25L) eliminate a separate pour step for licensed premises. The etched line is precise, subtle, and part of the glass — not a sticker or secondary tool. Operationally cleaner and audit-ready for LCRB inspection.
