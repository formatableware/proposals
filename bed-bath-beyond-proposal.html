Anfora Opal · Tempered Opal Glass · Venetian Tradition

Anfora — Frame Collection

The Frame collection by Anfora Opal is crafted from tempered opal glass — a material with the clean white appearance of fine porcelain and the thermal shock resistance of tempered glass. The defining detail is the collection’s precise rim architecture: a clean edge that creates a natural visual frame for any plating concept. Drawing on Venetian craft tradition, Frame covers the full dining programme from espresso to serving platters, all dishwasher safe and chip-resistant. A quietly distinctive choice for buyers who want something that reads as premium without announcing itself.

01
Tempered opal glass — porcelain appearance with superior thermal shock resistance, going from freezer to oven without cracking.
